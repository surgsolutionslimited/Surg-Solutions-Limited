## The Problem

This auto body repair shop had been running for years on referrals and word of mouth. They had a website — but it was doing nothing. In a full 6-month period, only 8 people found them through Google.

That means every single month, hundreds of people in their city were searching "panel beaters near me," "car respray," and "auto body repair" — and this business was invisible to all of them.

The owner was working hard. The business was good. But online, they didn't exist.

---

## What We Did

We built a complete local SEO system around their core services and location. This included:

- **Technical SEO audit and fixes** — cleaned up the site to reach a health score of 82/100
- **Local keyword targeting** — optimised pages for high-intent searches like "panel beaters," "headlight restoration," and "car respray" in their area
- **Location-specific content** — made sure Google understood exactly who they served and where
- **On-page optimisation** — structured every service page to rank for the right searches

No ad spend. No shortcuts. Just a properly built SEO foundation.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
