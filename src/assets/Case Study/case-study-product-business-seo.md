## The Problem

This product business had built a solid operation — good products, real customers, a functioning website. But their average Google ranking was position 22. That is deep on page 2, where almost no one looks.

They were getting traffic, but nowhere near what the business deserved. Competitors with weaker offerings were appearing above them simply because their SEO was better maintained.

The goal was clear: get to page 1, stay there, and turn search visibility into consistent business traffic.

## What a 91/100 Site Health Score Actually Means

Most business websites score between 50–70. A 91/100 score means:

- Google can crawl and index every page without obstacles
- Pages load quickly and correctly on mobile and desktop
- There are no broken links, duplicate content issues, or technical errors confusing search engines
- The site sends clean, consistent signals about what the business does and who it serves

A technically healthy site is the foundation everything else is built on. Without it, even great content and strong backlinks underperform.
