# How a Health Clinic Went from Page 2 to Page 1 on Google in 3 Months

**Industry:** Health & Wellness (Chiropractic Clinic)  
**Service:** Local SEO + Technical Optimisation  
**Timeline:** 3 months  
**Result:** Position 15 → 11, +17% traffic growth

---

## The Problem

This chiropractic clinic was already doing good work — patients were happy, reviews were positive, and the practice was established in its local area. But when potential new patients searched for a chiropractor nearby, this clinic was showing up at position 15 on Google.

Position 15 means page 2. And most people never reach page 2.

New patients who didn't already know about the clinic simply could not find it. Growth was limited to existing referral networks — and that ceiling was getting lower.

---

## What We Did

We deployed a local SEO strategy focused on making the clinic the most visible option in its area for chiropractic and related health searches. This included:

- **Local keyword optimisation** — targeted searches like "chiropractor [area]," "chiropractic clinic," and related service terms
- **Technical SEO cleanup** — addressed 14 technical errors that were quietly limiting rankings, bringing site health to 80/100
- **Service page optimisation** — structured each service page to clearly communicate relevance to both patients and Google
- **Local authority building** — strengthened the geographic signals telling Google exactly where this clinic operates

---

## The Results

In just 3 months, the clinic broke onto page 1.

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly clicks from Google | 218 | 256 | **+17.4%** |
| Average Google position | 15.5 | 11.6 | +3.9 positions |
| Click-through rate | 2.6% | 2.8% | +0.2% |

The primary location keyword — "chiropractor [area]" — moved to position 7, generating 21 clicks per month and continuing to climb. With further optimisation, moving this into the top 3 positions could double or triple those clicks.
